My Favorite Recipe: Beef Brocolli :D

I've tasted many recipes of beef brocolli from other restaurants, but I favor the way my dad cooks it the most. He always makes sure to balance its flavor, which led to me calling it as my favorite dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 pc white onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 300 g beef sirloin, sliced into strips
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 pc Knorr Beef Cubes
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 200 g broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch, dissolved in 1 to 2 tbsp water
>

Instructions

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat. Add canola oil and sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add beef and sear on both sides until browned.
  3. Add water into the pan and bring to a boil. Add Knorr Beef Cubes, oyster sauce, and broccoli. Stir well and simmer for 1 minute.
  4. Pour in dissolved cornstarch and simmer until sauce thickens. Serve hot.
